4. Do not grow attached to anyone or anything.


Humans easily grow attached to things, even ones not in their
reality. While it is their distinct trait, it leaves them
with many openings and weaknesses, while not gaining much.

5. Rely only on the power of your own self.


As with growing attached to things, relying on others is
another trait of humans. This, again, is not something to
be proud of. Relying creates weakness instead of the
strength one could gain from getting through a difficulty.

6. You are your own god.


There is no way to prove there even is a reality.
To simply put it, the only possibilities are that
you are perceiving a reality or creating your own.
In both cases, the end result, which is important
to you, is all in your head. This means that you
can shape reality through your own will, even if
this isn't an absolute power.

7. Losing sanity is the only way to absolute power.


As mentioned previously, the important part of reality
is in your mind. This fact means that the bounds you
yourself have are the bounds reality has. By simply
removing those bounds, you are free to shape reality
into the best one. The act of losing these "bounds"
is often acredited to losing one's sanity.

8. Worry not.


What you perceive as your entire reality, is incredibly miniscule
in the large picture. Most things do not matter with this view,
so worry not.
